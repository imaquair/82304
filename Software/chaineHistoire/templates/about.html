A Multicultural Experience

This project creates a participatory experience that invites and encourages members of the general public to interact with languages ​​they may not otherwise encounter in their daily lives. These languages ​​might include non-English native languages, languages people are learning, or even a language that is entirely new to them. Within the context of an American university campus, this project operates in an environment largely dominated by English, despite the fact that the majority of the student body is likely at least bilingual. Furthermore, current cultural and political trends have led to calls for designating English as the sole official language of the United States, exerting pressure to marginalize non-English languages ​and push them out of the public sphere. Consequently, this project aims to combat monolingualism and the hegemony of English by encouraging participants to engage in a collaborative act of storytelling and to inhabit a space that is multilingual, interlingual, and inclusive.

How-To

  1. Visit the Story Library and select a story that you want to contribute to. The language of the last entry is displayed for each story.
  2. Once you have chosen a story, you will see the entry from the previous story. Click the highlighted keywords to view their English translation. If a recording exists for the previous entry, it will be displayed as well.
  3. When you are ready to continue the story, start by enterring the language of your entry. This does not have to be the same language as the previous entry. Type or record your story continuation in your chosen language. If you choose to record your entry, your recording will be transcribed and displayed in the text input area. Feel free to edit the transcription to ensure it matches your recording.
  4. Once you have typed and/or recorded your entry, it will appear in the blue keyword selection region at the bottom of the page. Click on three words to translate them into English.
  5. Click Submit once you're done and come back later if you want to see the full finished story!
